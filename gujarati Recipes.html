Gujarati Recipes

Dhokla

Ingredients
  1. Gram flour (besan): 1 cup
  2. Semolina (rava): 2 tbsp
  3. Yogurt: ½ cup
  4. Water: ½ cup
  5. Turmeric powder: ¼ tsp
  6. Green chili-ginger paste: 1 tsp
  7. Fruit salt (Eno): 1 tsp
  8. Salt: to taste
  9. Sugar: 1 tbsp
  10. Oil: 1 tbsp
  11. Mustard seeds: 1 tsp
  12. Sesame seeds: 1 tsp
  13. Curry leaves: 10-12
  14. Green chilies: 2 (slit)
  15. Fresh coriander leaves: for garnishing
  16. Lemon juice: 1 tbsp

Cooking Instructions

  1. Prepare the Batter:
    1. In a mixing bowl, combine gram flour, semolina, yogurt, water, turmeric powder, salt, and sugar.
    2. Add green chili-ginger paste and mix well until you get a smooth batter. Let it rest for 15-20 minutes.
  2. Steaming the Dhokla:
    1. Grease a steaming tray or plate.
    2. Once the batter has rested, add fruit salt (Eno) and a few drops of water over it. Mix gently to incorporate.
    3. Pour the batter into the greased tray and steam for 15-20 minutes or until cooked through.
    4. Check with a toothpick; it should come out clean. Remove from heat and allow to cool slightly.
  3. Prepare the Tempering:
    1. Heat oil in a small pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
    2. Add sesame seeds, curry leaves, and slit green chilies. Fry for a few seconds.
    3. Add lemon juice and mix well.
  4. Final Steps:
    1. Pour the tempering over the steamed dhokla.
    2. Garnish with freshly chopped coriander leaves.
    3. Cut into squares or diamonds and serve.

Serve with: Green chutney or sweet tamarind chutney.
